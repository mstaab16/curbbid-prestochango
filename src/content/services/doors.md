## Doors That Just Work

A door that sticks every July is a door whose hinges have been telegraphing the same problem for years. Most fixes don't require a new door — just patient adjustment, a planed edge, a shimmed hinge, or a re-strike of the latch plate. Preston brings the right tools to do this on-site without a trip to the lumber yard.

## What Preston Handles

Interior door adjustments, latch and strike alignment, hinge tightening, and door slab replacement when a door is past saving. Exterior door installs (pre-hung) including new threshold and weatherstripping. Smart-lock installation — Schlage Encode, August, Yale Assure, etc. Re-keying matched residential cylinders so all your exterior doors take the same key. Patio sliding door track cleaning, roller replacement, and adjustment. Storm door installs.

## Weatherproofing for Texas Summers

Round Rock summers are no joke and a drafty exterior door bleeds money. Preston does new bulb-style weatherstripping, sweep replacement, threshold adjustment, and around-the-frame foam-and-caulk to seal the gap between the frame and rough opening. The result is usually noticeable on the AC bill within a billing cycle.

## Lockouts and Same-Day Re-keys

Lost a key or just bought the house? Preston can re-key matched residential cylinders the same day so every exterior door uses one new key, without waiting for a locksmith.
