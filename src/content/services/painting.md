## Small Paint Jobs Done Cleanly

Big paint jobs are for big paint crews. Presto Change-Os Handywork is built for the smaller stuff — one room, an accent wall, a hallway, all the trim and doors in a house, or a whole-home pre-listing refresh. The result: tight cut lines, even sheen, no rollover marks, and no painter's tape ripping the new paint off when it comes down.

## What's Included

Furniture moved or covered. Hardware removed (outlet covers, switch plates, door knobs) so paint never gets where it shouldn't. Holes patched and sanded. Surfaces cleaned. Two coats over a tinted primer when changing colors significantly. Crisp cuts at the ceiling, baseboard, and trim — by hand, not by tape. Cleanup at end of day, every day.

## Paint Brand and Color

Preston defaults to Sherwin-Williams Cashmere or Benjamin Moore Regal Select for walls (depending on what's already on them) and Advance or Emerald Urethane for trim and doors. If you prefer a different brand or already have paint on hand, that's fine — bring it on.

## Pre-Listing Refresh

Selling a house in the North Austin market? A targeted touch-up of trim, ceilings, and any color-loud bedrooms is one of the highest-return improvements before listing. Preston will walk through with you and quote only the rooms a buyer's first impression actually depends on.
