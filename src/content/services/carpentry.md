## Carpentry With a Magician's Patience

Trim is the part of a house that everyone sees and almost no one notices — until it's done badly. Presto Change-O's Handywork takes the time to scribe, cope, and miter so the joints actually disappear. No globs of caulk hiding sloppy cuts.

## What Preston Builds and Repairs

Baseboard, shoe molding, and quarter-round replacement. Crown molding repair and small new runs. Door hanging — pre-hung, slab, and pocket. Custom closet shelving and pantry organizers. Floating shelves anchored to studs (not toggle bolts that pull out the second you put a book on them). Mudroom benches, hook rails, and entry built-ins. Exterior trim and fascia repair, including rot replacement.

## Materials Done Right

For paint-grade work Preston uses primed finger-jointed pine or MDF where appropriate; for stain-grade he'll match species and grain direction. Fasteners get countersunk and filled, joints get glued, and everything gets primed before paint so the final coat actually looks like a finish — not a touch-up.
